comicbox.js

"A simple client-side JavaScript comic book archiving tool."

Series & Comics

New Series

Enter the title and year this series began e.g. "Superman" and "1939" to represent an entire series.

New Comic

Select the series (start typing it!) and then add an issue number to add that issue to your collection.

Your database will be saved to this browser and computer only. Deleting your cache (localStorage) will remove it for good.
